November Monthly Meeting

Organized by Igal Koshevoy

Time: Tuesday, November 6, 2007 from 6–8pm

Location: CubeSpace, CubeSpace, 622 SE Grand, Portland, OR

Agenda

The next Portland Ruby Brigade meeting is coming soon. If you’re doing
something interesting with Ruby, we’d be glad to have you make a
presentation or bring up an interesting topic for discussion.

I’ve reserved a spot at CubeSpace for the evening. We’ll be meeting at
the large open area in the cubes where they had the recent XPDX
presentation. The CubeSpace front desk staff will point you in the
right direction if needed. Directions to CubeSpace:
http://www.cubespacepdx.com/directions

I’ll talk with David of CubeSpace about reserving a regular spot for
us that evening. If you really want to have the meeting schedule
changed from the first Tuesday to something else, now would be a good
time to suggest an alternative.
