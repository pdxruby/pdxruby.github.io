March monthly meeting

Organized by Igal Koshevoy

Time: Tuesday, March 4, 2008 from 6–8pm

Location: CubeSpace, CubeSpace, 622 SE Grand, Portland, OR

Agenda

Join us for the next monthly meeting of the Portland Ruby Brigade. We don’t have any formal presentations lined up, so if you have something you’d like to present, please post to the mailing list. Otherwise, we’ll just meet up to do show-and-tell, sharing the projects we’re working on, asking for advice, and discussing Ruby.
