RailsConf 2008, Exhibition Hall Booth

Organized by Igal Koshevoy

Time: Friday, May 30, 2008 at 9:30am through Saturday, May 31, 2008 at 4:30pm

Location: Oregon Convention Center, 777 NE Martin Luther King, Jr. Blvd. Portland, Oregon 97232

Agenda

Join us at the Portland Ruby Brigade booth in the Oregon Convention Center’s Exhibition Hall. Local Rubyists will be on hand to talk about the area (e.g. recommend places to eat), demo and work on community-built projects, talk about Ruby and Rails, etc.

Please visit the sign-up spreadsheet to see who will be there and when.

Operating hours:

  • Friday: 9:30 – 10:45, 12:30 – 2:00, 3:30 – 4:30
  • Saturday: 10:00 – 10:45, 12:30 – 2:00, 3:30 – 4:30

Some members will also likely hang out at or near the Convention Center during the schedule breaks and afterwards, so please check the spreadsheet for latest details.

-igal
